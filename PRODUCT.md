# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Egyptian Artisans**: Professional and student craftspeople showcasing their handmade pottery, leatherwork, jewelry, textiles, and wood carvings. They need clear store management, milestone/escrow tracking, and a premium presentation.
- **Global Buyers**: Quality-conscious consumers looking for authentic, high-end handmade crafts. They expect a trustworthy, smooth purchase journey, clear pricing, security via escrow, and rich visual storytelling.
- **Platform Administrators**: Managers who oversee transactions, resolve disputes, curate featured listings, and support the community.

## Product Purpose
Curio is a specialized marketplace designed to connect local Egyptian artisans with a global audience. It aims to elevate traditional Egyptian crafts by providing a premium, secure SaaS-enabled marketplace experience with escrow-backed milestones, workshops, and AI-driven assistant tools.

## Brand Personality
- **Craft Luxury**: Sophisticated, authentic, and high-end. Reflects the depth of heritage and the precision of human hands.
- **Polished & Minimal**: Generous breathing room, crisp visual hierarchy, and subtle interactions. Avoids cluttered layouts.
- **Trustworthy**: Secure, clear, and reassuring, using clean alignments, solid typography, and structured transaction tracking.

## Anti-references
- **SaaS-gray / generic admin templates**: Avoid lifeless, flat dashboards with gray cards, high-density tables, and harsh borders.
- **E-commerce clutter**: Avoid endless blinking banners, crowded grid boxes, and overwhelming promotional alerts.
- **Rigid student-project borders**: Avoid harsh 1px borders surrounding every single element, causing visual boxes inside boxes.

## Design Principles
1. **Breathing Room (Visual Density = 4)**: Embrace generous negative space. Let high-resolution product photography and detailed descriptions breathe.
2. **Typography Hierarchy**: Use elegant Playfair Display for headings and highly legible Inter/Cairo for body copy, with strong weight and size contrasts.
3. **Subtle Elevation & Depth**: Use layered cards, soft shadows, and clean backdrop-filters instead of flat containers and hard borders.
4. **Snappy Micro-interactions (Motion Intensity = 6)**: Scale down slightly on press, stagger entry animations, and animate transitions to make the platform feel alive and responsive.
5. **Dual-Language Harmony**: Ensure layout structures remain visually balanced when toggling between English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG Level AA Contrast**: All body copy and interactive labels must maintain at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio against their backgrounds.
- **Prefers-reduced-motion Support**: Respect user system preferences by disabling/simplifying motion when requested.
- **Touch-friendly Targets**: Maintain clear click targets (minimum 44x44px) with fine pointer hover exclusions.
